Hot Honey Butter

Description

This delicious recipe is one of a kind. Spreading it across a warm bun will light up the christmas spirit within you.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons miced fresh sage leaves

Instructions:

  1. Add butter, hot sauce, red pepper flakes, and salt to a small bowl
  2. Use an electric mixer, and whip until butter is light and fluffy.
  3. Stir in honey and sage until well combined
  4. Transfer to an airtight container, and refrigerate for 1 hour

Voila! A Yummy Hot Honey Butter!
