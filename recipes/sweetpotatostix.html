Sweet Potato Stix

Description: This recipe will instruct you on how to make crispy, yummy sweet potato fries.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • Half a teaspoon of Paprika
  • 8 sweet potatoes, sliced lengthwise into quarters

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit, or 200 degrees Celsius
  2. Mix olive oil and paprika in a large bowl.
  3. Add potato sticks and stir by hand.
  4. Place on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes.

Voila ! Sweet Potato Stix
